Construction - Games Room
Introduction | Key Features | The Games | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

At level 30 Construction you can create your very own room full of games to play with your friends. From ranging to hangman, there is much fun to be had in the games room.

Key Features

A typical games room
A prize chest is found in the games room. Every game that is set up by the house owner will have the option 'Do you want to award a prize for this game'. If there is a prize in the chest (anyone can add coins to the chest), and prizes are set on, the winner will receive a key to the chest to claim the reward. Prizes can only be offered if the house owner is the person who starts the game.

The Games


Attack stones
These can be built in the centre of the games room. Here, you can challenge a friend to a friendly combat game. When set up, each player has a stone on the same place. You take it in turns to hit your stone, and the first one to destroy their stone is the winner. Beware! Bits of the stone can chip off and hurt you. The higher level the stone, the harder it is to damage.


Hoop and stick
This is the basic minigame for the Ranged game hotspot. Here you have ten shots to try to score higher than your opponents and win.


Darts
The second Ranged game you are able to build in this hotspot: use your Ranged skill to throw darts and score points. The player with the highest score after ten darts is the winner.


Archery target
This is the highest level Ranged game you can build. Here, players have to shoot ten arrows or bolts at the target. The highest score after ten shots is declared the winner.


Jester
When activated, a jester is summoned. When they appear they will start the game. Simple and fun: the first player to copy ten emotes correctly will be declared the winner.


Treasure hunt
When this game is activated, a fairy will give all players a magic stone. She will then hide somewhere, in the house. Your job is to feel the stone she has given you. Every time you feel the stone, it will update from the last time you felt it. If the stone gets colder, you need to try the other direction. If it gets warmer, you are going the right way. When it gets hot you are close to the fairy. If it is very close, move slowly, feeling the stone to see where the fairy is hidden.


Hangman
When activated, you take it in turns to guess a letter missing from a word to do with RuneScape. If you guess incorrectly, the game builds up until the puppet gets hung! The winner is the person who first guesses the word correctly.

There are two ways to do this: be the person to guess the last missing letter correctly; if you know the answer in advance, you can click the 'guess' button. You then enter up to five letters you think are missing, these letters then get entered into the puzzle.


Elemental balance
This game requires logic and cunning in using Magic to solve puzzles. A sphere will rise when the game is initiated. Anyone who casts spells on the sphere is involved in the game and must take it in turns. The aim is to get the colour to white so the elements are balanced.

Elements here refer to water, earth, air and fire. Water and fire are opposed, as are earth and air. Hence, if the colour of the sphere is red, you would be wise to cast a water spell on it. Examining the sphere shows how much of the colour it contains. Dependent on the amount of colour it has in it, you might want to consider your spellbook. For example: hitting a red sphere with water wave will add more blue to it than using water strike/blast. Using this logic, you must cast in turns until someone balances the sphere to become the winner.

The Hotspots

Games room hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are six types of hotspot in the games room that you can construct on:

  • Stone
  • Elemental balance
  • Game
  • Storage
  • Ranging game
  • Room
