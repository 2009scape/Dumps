Melee Weapons


Daggers, swords, axes, pickaxes and hatchets can all be smithed from the ore you find in the dungeons of Daemonheim. Simply smelt the ore at a furnace (often you will have to light furnaces with wood and a tinderbox) and then use the resulting bar at an anvil, which can often found in the starting room. Hammers and tinderboxes can be bought from the smuggler in the starting room.

Primal weapons (tier 11) can only be gained from monster drops. Flameburst defenders, frostbite daggers and hailstorm daggers can all be gained as drops from slayer creatures.

Melee Armour

The armour of Daemonheim can be smithed from the ore you find in its dungeons. Simply smelt ore at a furnace (often you will have to light furnaces with wood and a tinderbox) and then use the resulting bar at an anvil, which can often found in the starting room. Hammers and tinderboxes can be bought from the smuggler in the starting room. Depending on the complexity level, it is also likely that you will receive melee armour when you first enter a dungeon.

Primal armour (tier 11) can only be gained from monster drops. Silver precision bracelets, sunstriker boots, golden precision bracelets, marksman boots, protector's wards and guardian's wards can all be gained as drops from slayer creatures.
