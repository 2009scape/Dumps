1. How does the Paymo payment method work?

To pay using Paymo, you enter your mobile phone number into our website, and you receive a text message confirming the price of your subscription. You then reply to the text message to confirm your payment. This will provide you with one month of membership. The cost of your subscription will be added to your mobile phone bill if you have a contract, otherwise it will be taken from the credit on your phone. Your network provider may also apply standard text messaging charges.

2. How much does it cost?

The cost of membership varies depending on the charges applied by your network.

3. Which networks are supported?

You can use Paymo with Alltel, AT&T, Boost, Cellular One, Nextel, Sprint, T-Mobile, US Cellular, Verizon and Virgin.

4. What countries is this payment method available in?

Currently, Paymo is available only to players from the USA, UK, Canada, Australia and Germany.

5. How long will it take for my membership to start?

Your membership will begin as soon as your payment has been processed. Please allow a few minutes for this.

6. How do I make a payment?

From the Account Management page, click 'Start/Extend Subscription', and follow the prompts for using the Paymo payment method. Please be sure to follow the instructions and make sure you understand all the points.

7. How do I cancel my membership?

Payment will be taken only when you send a message to the Paymo service. If you no longer wish to continue membership on your account, simply do not resubscribe.

8. How many months of membership can I buy at a time?

You can buy one month of membership at a time.

9. How many payments will be taken?

Only one payment will be taken each time you use the Paymo service.

10. The Paymo service is not available in my country. What can I do?

We are always looking to add new payment options for our players. If Paymo is not currently available in your country, please do not submit a query regarding this. Instead, keep an eye on our Billing methods, prices and partners page for any new billing options. In the meantime, you may wish to consider one of the other payment methods we offer.
