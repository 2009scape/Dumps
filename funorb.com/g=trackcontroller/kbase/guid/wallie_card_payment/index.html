1. How does the Wallie Card payment method work?

Wallie Cards are a prepaid payment method, so when you have purchased a Wallie Card you can use it to pay for FunOrb membership.

2. What countries is this payment method available in?

Wallie Cards may be used to pay for membership in the following countries:

  • Belgium
  • Germany
  • Latvia
  • Mexico
  • Netherlands
  • Spain
  • Sweden
  • United Kingdom

3. How secure is this payment method?

Wallie Cards are purchased from an independent company who take great care with your payment details and enable you to pay for other services without revealing any information. You can also purchase them from retailers in some towns and cities, so your details may never be attached to your Wallie Card.

4. How much does it cost?

The cost depends on the currency you use to purchase your Wallie Card.

5. How do I subscribe or make a one-off payment?

Select 'Subscribe to FunOrb' on the FunOrb front page (under the 'Join' drop-down menu)and select the prompts for using the Wallie Card payment method.

Please be sure to follow the instructions and ensure you understand all the points.

6. How do I unsubscribe?

All payments are one-off, non-recurring payments, so there is no need to unsubscribe from any service.

7. How long will it take for my membership to start?

The membership credit will be added to the account as soon as the payment has been processed. This is normally instant.

8. What should I include with my payment?

Details of exactly what to provide with your payment will be noted during the subscription process.

9. I no longer wish to pay for a subscription. What should I do?

You do not enter into a contract until you pay for a new subscription. If you have changed your mind and no longer wish to subscribe using Wallie Card payments, simply do nothing.

10. I have sent my payment, but I�m still not a member. How long will it take?

First of all, please check your Wallie Card account for the payment. If it is present and you are still not a member, then contact us, here; otherwise, please contact Wallie Card.

11. How many months of membership credit can I buy at a time?

You can purchase 1, 3, 6 and 12 months of credit at any one time. Promotional Wallie Cards allow access to 7 days of free membership.

12. How many payments will be taken?

Only one payment will be taken if you subscribe using a Wallie Card.

13. I have some credit left over on my Wallie Card. Can I use it for future payments?

You can use up to five Wallie Cards to cover the cost of a single transaction. Once you have entered your first code, you will be given the option to add more credit before completing the payment.
